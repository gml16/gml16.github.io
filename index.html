ChangeXChange

Gone are the days of arriving in a country penniless, feeling scammed, or having cumbersome foreign change at home; ChangeXChange is here for you. Meet up with travellers on the opposite leg of their journey to either pick up the foreign currencies they came back with, or trade them yours. With easy to use filters, and automatic airport detection, look for, or create an offer from one currency to another, and find a fellow globetrotter with matching needs.

On a technical note, two friends and I implemented this Android app in Java using design patterns from the Gang of Four and agile methods.
